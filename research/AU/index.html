Research

Auburn University

REU on Smart UAVs

I spent the summer of 2017 at Auburn University in Auburn, Alabama working at the REU for Smart Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). My research partner James Brown and I took a machine learning approach to the problem of snapshot-based visual homing. We used homography control law to compute the direction vector for the UAV to navigate home. We used Python and MATLAB to write code for the convolutional neural network and simulations. Our research is explained in our paper draft, final oral presentation, and final poster presentation.

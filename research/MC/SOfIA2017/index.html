SOfIA 2017

During the summer of 2017, I participated in the SOfIA program as a returning student mentor for the Analog and Digital Sound Synthesis project. After some preliminary work with mapping the sound space of the LittleBits Synth Kit, the group used the LittleBits components to explore frequency modulation (FM) sythesis with non-sinusoidal carrier waves.

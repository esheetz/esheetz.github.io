SOfIA 2014

During the summer of 2014, I participated in the Summer Opportunities for Intellectual Activity (SOfIA) project, a three week research experience through Monmouth College focused on introducing freshmen to research. I worked on the High Speed Imagery and Mathematical Modelling project, in which we modelled the popping of a soap bubble.
